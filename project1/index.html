Environmental Safety

Humans Beings are the major cause for the destruction of the resources in the environment.We all require the sources from Environment,but we are not using them in a proper way and we are all taking more than enough source from environment.It damages a lot to our surrounding and there will be scarcity to the future generations.Majorly we all use private transportaion which spoils the air causes air polltion and increase in toxic gases in atmosphere.CFCs from our Resfrigirators and AC leads to Global Warming.Throwing plastic materials into water puts aquatic life in danger and causes water pollution...etc and there are many other causes that destroys our environment.So it is very much require to save our environment.Thats the reason to start a Environment protection cell which organises a lot of people to protest against the environmental tragadies.Our major activities are

  • Collecting funds to save wildlife during any environmental tragadies like forest fires.
  • Protest against the MNCs which dont follow the minimum environmantal safety measures.
  • Engaing in social activities like 'Swatch Bharath' in order to clean the surroundings.
  • Planting Trees in the public areas during weekends

Our cell is active in many major cities like Hyderabad,Mumbai,Delhi,Kolkata,Chennai..etc in India.
